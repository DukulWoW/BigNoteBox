# About
BigNoteBox is a fully-featured in-game notepad addon for World of Warcraft (12.0). It lets you create, organize, and surface notes without ever leaving the game. Whether you are writing down a boss strategy, tracking quest objectives, jotting down crafting materials, or keeping notes on a player, BNB gets it in front of you at the right moment.
